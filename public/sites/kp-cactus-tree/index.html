What folks call us for first

Saguaros, the right way

A saguaro is protected, heavy, and unforgiving if it comes down wrong. Kevin's crew takes the time to do it carefully, cutting a leaning section in pieces so falling debris does not damage a nurse tree or a young cactus below, tagging and relocating the big ones, and saving the cactus whenever it can be saved.

  • Pruning a leaning or top heavy saguaro, taken down in sections
  • Removing a dying or unsafe cactus close to the house, then hauling it off
  • Relocating cactus around your yard, tagged properly on the larger ones
