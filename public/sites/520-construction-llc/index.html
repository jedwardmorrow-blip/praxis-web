How we build

A slab is only as good as what's under it

Plenty of cracked patios start with a skipped step. We do the unglamorous part right, then form it, pour it, and finish it clean. Here's the order every 520 job follows.

1

Prep & grade

We strip, grade and compact a solid base so the concrete sits level and stays put. This is the step that decides whether it lasts.

2

Form & pour

Clean forms, steel where it counts, and an even pour. We set the slopes so water runs off the way it should, not toward your house.

3

Finish & clean up

Broom, trowel, pavers or flagstone, the finish you asked for, edges crisp. Then the site gets cleaned up before we leave, every time.
