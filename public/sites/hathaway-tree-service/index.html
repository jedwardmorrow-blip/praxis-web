What sets Collin apart

Big removals over fences, roofs and patios.

A tree pressed up against the house is not a job for a bucket truck and a chainsaw from the ground. Collin climbs in, ropes the limbs, and lowers each piece where it can do no harm, then grinds the stump and clears the brush.
