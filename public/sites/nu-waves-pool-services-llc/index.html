What I do

From a green mess to a system that runs right.

Most pool companies want to skim and leave. I do that too, well, but the reason people stick with Nu Waves is the harder stuff: pumps, filters, and lights that finally work, diagnosed straight and fixed once.

The specialty
★ What sets Nu Waves apart

Equipment repair & diagnostics

Pumps, filters, valves, automation and pool lights. I find what's actually wrong before anything gets replaced, give you options in a few price ranges, and fix it so it lasts. No guessing, no upselling.

  • Pumps & motors
  • Filters & valves
  • Pool lights
  • Leak & flow diagnosis

Weekly pool & spa service

Brushed, skimmed, baskets emptied, and a full panel chemistry test with the right dosing every visit, so your water stays clear and balanced without you thinking about it.

Filter service & replacement

DE, cartridge and sand filters cleaned, rebuilt or swapped. If your filter is pushing pressure or pushing dirt back into the pool, that's a quick win I see all the time.

Resurface & fresh start

When a pool is past balancing, we can drain it and start clean, new surface, fresh water, equipment dialed in. The reset that makes an old pool feel new again.

For the DIY owner

Want to run your own pool? I'll teach you.

Not everyone wants a weekly service, and that's fine. Sit down with me one on one and I'll walk you through your equipment, your chemistry, and what to actually watch for, so you can take care of your pool with confidence instead of guesswork.
