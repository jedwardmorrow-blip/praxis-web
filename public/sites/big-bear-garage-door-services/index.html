What we fix

The calls we get most

Most of our work is the stuff that strands you: a spring that lets go like a gunshot, a door that jumped the track, an opener that finally quit. Here is what we handle.

Broken springs

The most common emergency. We carry torsion springs and swap them safely so your door lifts smooth again, same visit when we can.

Most common call

Snapped cables

Frayed or broken lift cables leave the door crooked or stuck. We replace them and re-balance both sides so it seals flat to the ground.

Door won't close evenly

Off the rails

Backed into it, or it jumped the track? We straighten the section, reset the rollers and get the door back on its tracks and moving.

Came off the track

Openers, installed

New opener or a dead one replaced, remotes and keypad programmed, everything tested before we leave. We can match what you already have, including Chamberlain.

Install & replace

Rollers & rebuild

Loud, jerky, squealing doors usually need rollers, brackets and bracing. We quiet the whole system down so you forget it is even opening.

Quiet it down

Tune-ups & alignment

Adjustments, lubrication, better bracing and a safety check. A door that closes evenly keeps the heat, the dust and the mice out.

Keep it running
