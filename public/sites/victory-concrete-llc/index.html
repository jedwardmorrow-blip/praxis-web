The finish

The slab is only as good as the prep

Anybody can dump concrete. Holding a slab flat, square, and crack-resistant in the Tucson heat comes from the work nobody sees: the base, the forms, the steel, and getting the finish timed to the cure. That is where Victory spends its time.

  • Compacted base and graded forms before a single yard is poured
  • Rebar and wire set on chairs, not laid flat on the dirt
  • Control joints cut on a plan so the slab cracks where you want it to
  • Edges, borders, and finish chosen with you before pour day
  • Site swept and hauled clean when the crew leaves
