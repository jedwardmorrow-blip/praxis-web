What we do

Water first, then everything around it.

Browning's is built around irrigation, but the name says "and more" for a reason. Once the water's right, the same crew can refresh the whole yard.

The water

Irrigation core

New irrigation installs

Drip and spray systems laid out for desert plants and real Tucson water bills, designed so every plant actually gets its share.

Repairs & dry spots

Broken heads, cracked lines, a zone that quit, a corner going brown. We track down why and fix it the same visit when we can.

Timers & controller boxes

Installed, swapped, or just re-set for the season so you're not watering at noon in July or flooding the drive at 3am.

Drip & sprinkler redesigns

Adding beds, pulling grass, switching to low-water drip. We re-route the system to match the yard you actually want.
