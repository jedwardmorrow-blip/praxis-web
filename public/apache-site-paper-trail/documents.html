Fair questions
Will our customers notice a difference?

Same documents, better outcomes: verified addresses, faster delivery, and email or portal options for customers who prefer digital. The letterhead is still yours.

How do we know what it would save?

Bring one month of statements or notices. We map today's real cost — including the staff hours — against the workflow, per piece. The math decides, not the pitch.

What about time-sensitive or compliance mail?

Those are the pieces that benefit most: certified handling where needed, delivery records for everything, and an archive you can actually search.

Is this like using a print shop?

No. A print shop runs your twice-a-year advertising job. This is your everyday operational mail — invoices, statements, notices, legal and healthcare documents — with security, address verification, and delivery records built in. It can do marketing mail too, but transactional mail is the point.

Is a four-person local company really equipped for this?

The rails are enterprise-grade Quadient infrastructure — the same platform processing mail at national scale, routing each job to the production facility closest to its recipients. Apache configures it, sells it honestly, and answers for it locally. Big-company machinery, local accountability.
