Fair questions
When should we look at our meter lease?

Twelve to eighteen months before it ends. Renewal terms often lock earlier than offices expect, and the notice usually arrives quietly by mail. Bring the lease and your last invoice — reading the dates takes twenty minutes.

What if our mail volume is small?

Then right-sizing is the whole point of the review. Sometimes the honest answer is smaller equipment, different equipment, or none at all — you'll get that answer either way.

How do supplies work?

Order by phone, email, or web, or set up automatic low-ink replenishment so statement day never starts with an empty cartridge.
