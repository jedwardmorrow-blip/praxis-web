Apache website · shot list

Nine photos make this site unmistakably Apache.

Shoot on a phone in good light — honest beats polished for a 60-year local business. Landscape orientation for everything. Avoid vendor logos filling the frame; the subject is always Apache people, Apache equipment, Apache Tucson.

How the swap works: save each photo as a JPG named exactly like its slot (for example slot-01-team.jpg) and drop it into assets/photos/ inside the site folder. Every placeholder on the site finds its photo and replaces itself automatically — no code edits, no rebuild. Shots 1–6 are wired today; shots 7–9 are worth capturing on the same visit for launch and Google Business Profile use.
