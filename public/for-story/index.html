Role Signal

Two postings. One learning system.

The postings do not read like novelty tooling. The job language points to the same core problem from two angles: AI has to reduce manual work, improve decision quality, protect operating controls, and become usable team behavior.

AI Enablement Manager

Adoption has to become operating behavior.

The posting centers on enterprise-wide adoption, internal consulting, workflow optimization, responsible usage, documentation, training, and measurable operational impact.

My read

Start with high-friction workflows, pilot narrowly with the teams who own the work, document the behavior, then measure whether speed, visibility, and execution actually improve.

Accounting & Finance AI Automation

Default-to-AI still needs audit discipline.

The finance role points at automation, data integrity, scalable reporting, faster decisions, financial controls, close process improvement, and systems that can operate across entities.

My read

Use AI to surface exceptions, prepare evidence, and reduce cleanup work while keeping finance ownership, source-of-truth discipline, and human review intact.

Why cannabis matters

The hard part is the handoff.

In cannabis, the model is rarely the hardest part. The friction lives in cultivation reality, inventory drift, retail execution, compliance nuance, finance pressure, and team adoption.

My read

Treat cultivation, production, inventory, retail, finance, and training as connected operating signals instead of isolated tools or one-off automations.

AI Daily Brief lens

The tool is not the moat. The learning loop is.

The strongest AI program captures how work actually happens, turns repeated judgment into reusable memory, and lets each pilot make the next pilot smarter without weakening controls.

First 30 Days

One plan. Four chapters.

01

02

Rank the learning loops

Prioritize improvements by operational impact, compliance sensitivity, data readiness, repeatability, and adoption potential.

03

Pilot with controls

Implement practical AI workflows with clear success criteria, human review, source-of-truth discipline, and feedback loops.

04

Train, measure, compound

Build resources, SOPs, and adoption metrics so each proven pilot improves speed, visibility, execution, and the next pilot.
